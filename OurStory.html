Our Story

How It All Began

Develup was first a thought in a dorm room. Two friends who both had their own personal struggles with the college application process discussed the imperfections and misleading information that high school students transitioning to the college lifestyle often experienced. So they decided to try to make a change for the students that were soon to follow them into college.

After working on a solution for months, they decided to launch Develup. A transparent, low-cost, personalized college preparation package for incoming college students who, not only want a head start on their peers heading into college, but also want to succeed the moment they step onto campus.
